About The Author

Kader Arnold was born in Bryn Mawr, Pennsylvania- initially under the name Abdul Quadir Muhaiyadeeen Arnold. When he was 6 years old his parents decided to change his name to the phoenetic pronunciation of the arabic name (rhymes with water). Kader is an inventor, investor, and entrepeneur and recieved little formal schooling. He is versed predominantly in unix-based operating systems, full-stack web development, the C programming language, music theory, composition, and the like. Kader also has interests along the lines of Wine, Restaurenteurs, and microeconomic catalysts.
